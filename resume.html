Here is my story

It started with design

Fell in love with usability, accessibility and aesthetics.

Volunteered at a school for the blind and visually impaired, studied sports science and taught there. Was able to notice the impact of design and usability in everyday life for my students.

Had the opportunity to join a startup, importing and renting designer furniture. We became the largest designer furniture importer in Brazil.

While managing the company we used excel, and experienced the pitfalls of that workflow. But, as a small company, we couldn't spend time, or hire someone to develop us a system.

That was when I started to automate and develop solutions in excel using macros and VBA.

Pandemic hit, and the hospitality business collapsed.

Decided to use the last year to dive deeper into software development

And now I'm applying my eye for design and innovation, my adaptability, and experience to develop software solutions.
